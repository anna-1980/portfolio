About me:

Hello,

I was always passionate about discovering new things, solving problems, answering questions and learning. Out of this passion a scientist was born. I joined University and started training my research skills in biology. Eventually this earned me a PhD title. The pursuit of knowledge, learning teaching I find all very fascinating but slowly my interest drifted. Whenever I would present my results or teach student I would put more and more of my science into pictures.


Eventually I taught myself more and more in computer Graphics and Science Illustration. In this way I realized my place is not at the bench anymore, as I started drawing more. I started looking for a job which could combine better my scientific knowledge and drive to show science in pictures.


Meanwhile, at some point I also desired my own space in the world wide web - a website. As the usual me I thought I can just do it myself, there are so many resources online. At this point however, I did not know how to code... Luckily there are awesome programs which can teach you Web Development in just a few months. I enrolled, and guess what? I loved it! I even learned an interesting frame work (Phaser) which helps you to code HTML games quicker. Of course, I immediately thought - Why not educational games :) So here I am, today, happily coding :D
